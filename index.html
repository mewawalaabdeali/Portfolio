Hi! I'm Abde Ali,
AI Engineer - Bridging Cloud Infrastructure with Generative Intelligence

I’m a Cloud and DevOps Engineer by experience, and an AI Engineer by choice — with 7+ years of hands-on experience building scalable systems, cloud automation pipelines, and backend architectures in India’s tech ecosystem. Over the years, my journey evolved from infrastructure to intelligence, where I now specialize in bridging cloud-native engineering with modern AI workflows. I build systems that automate data ingestion, cleaning, and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) using tools like LangChain, LangGraph, PyTorch, OpenAI, Gemini, SQL, Streamlit, and Docker. My focus is on building real-world, production-grade AI tools — not just models, but intelligent systems that think, adapt, and scale. Currently, I’m pushing the boundaries at the intersection of Agentic/Generative AI/ML systems and software engineering, aspiring to grow into roles like AI Engineer, MLOps Engineer, or Software Engineer with an AI flavor, where both my infrastructure past and AI-driven present shape the systems of tomorrow.
